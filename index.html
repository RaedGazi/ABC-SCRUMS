Letter A

A is for Amber. It is fossilized sap from trees from prehistoric times. Sometimes there are insects and plants inside!

Letter B

B is for Blueberries. A tasty fruit that is used in a ton of smoothies and pastries!

Letter C

C is for Citrine. It is a very popular gemstone with a deep orange color and is the birthstone for November!

Letter J

J is for Jellyfish. Funny looking sea animals found in the sea! Watch out for the tentacles, they hurt!

Letter K

K is for Kangaroo. An animal that loves to jump around!

Letter L

L is for Lion. The king of the jungle and one of the most recognizable large cats in the world
